My Week


Once the semester begins, the days reshape themselves — not rushed, but carefully arranged.
There is planning in the margins, emails folded like paper cranes, lectures stacked in rows, and self-care flexed gently between the lines.
Each day becomes a layout: columns of focus, rows of effort, spaced just enough to breathe.
Some moments stretch, others align — and like Flexbox, I learn to grow within the container I'm in.
With Grid, I find order.
With Flex, I find flow.
In both, I build a rhythm, a routine, a life redesigned in code and care.
Mine.
